EQUASHIELD’s fully encapsulated Syringe Unit prevents Syringe plunger contamination caused by hazardous​ Drugs handing. The system is also equipped with a built-in closed pressure equalization system with factory sealed ​ Sterile air chamber. The easy- to –use single motion connector remains dry after use as it prevents leaks and drug ​ Residuals on outer membrane surfaces. Additionally, the connector is factory welded to the syringe saving valuable ​ Setup time compared to time spent on opening two package and assembling a connector onto an off the shelf syringe.

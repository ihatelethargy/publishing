EQUASHIELD’s syringe units are fully encapsulated to prevent syringe plunger contamination caused by hazardous drug handling. The system has a built-in closed pressure that is the same of sterile air. the connector is permanently bonded onto the syringe body preventing accidental disconnections and house shielded needles to prevent risk of accidental needle sticks.​

EQUASHIELD’s spike adaptors are used to safely transfer medication dose into the IV bag and designed to connect between any standard IV bag and tubing set firmly. Now we provide SA-1T (regular ports) and SA-1 (lager ports) as the spike adaptors because there are variations among various IV Bag manufacturers, and that no one IV port fits all bags. Only the SA-W is for withdrawing diluent safely and cost-effectively to reconstitute syringe units.

EQUASHIELD Spike Adaptor firmly connects between any standard IV bag and a tubing set. The Spike Adaptor enables safe ​ Injection of drugs into the IV bag using a Syringe Unit and subsequent administration via any tubing set.​ Recognizing that there are variations among various IV Bag manufactures and that no one IV port fits all bags, Equashied​ Produces two Spike Adaptors : SA-1T for regular ports and SA-1 for bags with larger ports. The SA-1 Port design prevents​ The opportunity of accidental spike disconnection from IV bag reducing an opportunity for leak and spills​ An additional Spike Adaptor (Ref: SA –W ) is designed to be used with the Syringe Unit to withdraw diluents, such as​ Normal saline or sterile water, from an IV bag​

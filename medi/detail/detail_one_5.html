EQUASHIELD Pro is industry's first CSTD Automated Compounding Machine. Equashield® Pro relies on the Equashield's proprietary CSTD to redefine the automation workflow to optimize throughput. The system enables compounding a large variety of patient specific chemotherapy doses quickly, using a unique and safe method of compounding that shortens the process flow while performing multiple tasks simultaneously.
